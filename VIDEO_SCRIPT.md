# RecipeNest 5-Minute Video Presentation Script 🎬

This script is designed to help you showcase the project effectively for your markers.

---

### **0:00 - 0:45: Introduction & Branding**
- **Action**: Show the Home Page and scroll through the hero and featured sections.
- **Script**: "Hello everyone! This is RecipeNest, a premium full-stack chef portal designed to bridge the gap between world-class chefs and home cooks. As you can see, we've built a modern, responsive landing page using Tailwind v4, focusing on a high-end culinary aesthetic."

### **0:45 - 1:30: Discovery & Search**
- **Action**: Click 'Recipes' in the Navbar. Search for 'Momo' or click a Category pill.
- **Script**: "The platform features a powerful discovery engine. Users can search by title or ingredients and filter by categories like Dinner, Breakfast, or Vegan. Our backend uses MongoDB for flexible data storage, allowing for rapid search and real-time updates."

### **1:30 - 2:30: Recipe Details & User Engagement**
- **Action**: Click on a recipe. Show the Ingredients, Instructions, and the Review section.
- **Script**: "Each recipe details page provides a comprehensive breakdown of prep time, servings, and step-by-step instructions. Authenticated users can also save recipes to their bookmarks or share their experience by leaving a star rating and comment."

### **2:30 - 3:30: Chef Portfolio & Dashboard**
- **Action**: Click on a Chef's name. Then log in as a Chef and show the Chef Dashboard.
- **Script**: "For our chefs, the platform serves as a professional portfolio. Chefs have a dedicated dashboard where they can track their culinary stats and manage their recipes. Let's look at the 'Add Recipe' form — it's fully functional, allowing chefs to upload images, define ingredients, and publish instantly."

### **3:30 - 4:30: Admin Controls & Statistics**
- **Action**: Log out and log in as an Admin. Show the Admin Dashboard stats and the 'Manage Recipes' table.
- **Script**: "Finally, we have the Administrative panel. Admins get a bird's-eye view of platform performance through real-time statistics. They can manage all users, recipes, and categories, ensuring the platform remains high-quality and safe for everyone."

### **4:30 - 5:00: Technical Summary & Conclusion**
- **Action**: Briefly show the code structure (Controllers or Services) if possible.
- **Script**: "Technically, the app is powered by an ASP.NET Core API with JWT security and BCrypt hashing. We've implemented DTOs and the Service pattern for a clean, professional architecture. Thank you for watching!"
